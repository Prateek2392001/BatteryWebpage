Li-ion Battery

We offer advanced lithium-ion batteries that provide high energy density, faster charging, and longer life cycles—ideal for electric vehicles, energy storage, and consumer electronics.

Prismatic Cell

Prismatic cells are compact and space-efficient, making them a preferred choice for large battery packs in electric vehicles and high-capacity energy storage systems.

Telecom Service

Our battery solutions ensure reliable backup power for telecom towers and infrastructure, maintaining seamless communication even in off-grid or emergency situations.

Integrated Battery Monitoring System

Our smart battery management system continuously monitors performance, temperature, and charge levels to enhance safety, extend battery life, and enable predictive maintenance.

Cylindrical Cell

Cylindrical cells offer excellent mechanical stability and thermal performance, making them ideal for modular battery designs used in power tools, medical devices, and e-bikes.

Industrial and Commercial

Our energy solutions are designed to meet the demanding needs of industrial and commercial applications, delivering high efficiency, durability, and safety for continuous operations.
